Welcome to Nimble!¶

Nimble is built on top of Python’s most popular data science and machine learning libraries to provide a single, easy to use, API for any data science job. We believe the best way to learn about Nimble is to see it in action. So, we invite you to check out our cheatsheet and the examples below and explore how Nimble makes data science easier.
